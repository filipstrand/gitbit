Devicon
=======

This project bundles a curated subset of Devicon SVGs under `media/icons/devicon-*.svg`.

Devicon is MIT-licensed (see the Devicon project). Devicon also notes that product names,
logos, and brands are property of their respective owners and are used for identification
purposes only; usage does not imply endorsement.
